What Makes stainless steel security screens Different?

stainless steel security screens isn't just another security screen. It's an engineered security system designed and manufactured in Brisbane, built to protect Australian homes against forced entry, extreme weather, and insects.

Patented mechanical clamping™ Technology

Unlike traditional security screens that rely on a rubber spline to hold the mesh in place, stainless steel security screens mechanically clamps the mesh into the frame using screws and a clamp bar. This creates a connection that cannot be pushed, pulled, or kicked out under impact — the key reason stainless steel security screens outperforms every competitor.

Tested Beyond AS 5039

Australian Standard AS 5039 sets the minimum requirements for security screens. stainless steel security screens is tested well beyond these requirements, including dynamic impact, knife shear, jemmy resistance, and salt spray corrosion tests. It doesn't just pass — it exceeds.

Tensile Tuff® Mesh

stainless steel security screens uses 316 marine grade stainless steel mesh with 0.9mm wire diameter. This Tensile Tuff mesh is woven to be strong yet fine enough to maintain clear visibility and airflow. It won't rust, corrode, or deteriorate in Brisbane's coastal and subtropical climate.

Brisbane-Designed, Australian-Made

stainless steel security screens was invented in Brisbane and continues to be manufactured in Australia. Every product is purpose-built for Australian conditions — from tropical humidity to bushfire zones. As an authorised dealer, we supply genuine stainless steel security screens backed by the full manufacturer warranty.
