How Much Do Security Screens Cost in Brisbane?

Complete 2026 pricing guide for security doors, fly screens, Crimsafe, blinds, and full-house packages.

By Mark Flynn | Published April 2026 | 12 min read

By Mark Flynn — 25+ years in the security screen industry

Owner, All Seasons Screens & Blinds • Capalaba, QLD

Quick Answer

Security screen doors in Brisbane range from $400 for a basic aluminium grille to $2,800+ for a premium Crimsafe Ultimate. The average Brisbane homeowner spends $5,000–$10,000 for a full house with quality stainless steel mesh screens on all doors and windows.

If you're looking to secure your Brisbane home, the first question is always about cost. Prices vary widely depending on the type of mesh, brand, door or window size, colour, and lock options. In this guide, we break down every product category with real 2026 pricing so you can budget confidently before getting a quote.

All prices below are typical installed prices in the Brisbane and Redlands area. Your exact cost will depend on your home's measurements and specifications — that's why we always recommend a free measure and quote.

What Affects the Price?

Nine key factors determine what you'll pay for security screens in Brisbane:

1. Mesh Type

Aluminium grille is cheapest. Stainless steel 316 mesh is mid-range. Crimsafe Tensile Tuff is premium.

2. Brand

Crimsafe, Prowler Proof, and Amplimesh command a premium for patented technology and warranties.

3. Size

Oversized doors and wide windows cost more. Bi-fold and stacking systems multiply per panel.

4. Colour

Standard colours (black, white, grey) are cheapest. Custom powder-coat colours add $50-$150 per item.

5. Locks

Standard single-point locks are included. Triple-lock systems add $80-$200 for extra security.

6. Extras

Pet doors, vision-restricted mesh, bushfire-rated mesh, and self-closing hinges all add cost.

7. Quantity

Doing the whole house at once is better value per unit than one door at a time. Ask about package deals.

8. Access

Multi-storey homes or difficult access (e.g., scaffolding needed) increase installation cost.

9. Old Screen Removal

Removing and disposing of old screens is usually included, but check — some installers charge extra.

Is It Worth It? ROI Calculation

Security screens are one of the few home improvements that actually pay for themselves. Here's the maths for a typical mid-range full-house installation at $7,500:

Insurance premium savings $100 – $300/year
Energy bill reduction (cooling) ~$120/year
Property value increase +$10,000 – $20,000
Annual savings (conservative) ~$320/year
Over 15-year lifespan $4,800 in savings + property uplift

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a Crimsafe security door cost in Brisbane?
A Crimsafe Regular hinged security door in Brisbane typically costs $1,200–$2,000+ installed. The Crimsafe Ultimate model ranges from $1,800–$2,800+ depending on door size, colour, and lock options. Sliding Crimsafe doors start around $1,500. Prices include professional measure, supply, and installation.
How much does it cost to screen a whole house in Brisbane?
A full-house security screen package in Brisbane ranges from $3,000–$5,000 for basic aluminium grille screens, $5,000–$10,000 for mid-range stainless steel mesh on all doors and windows, and $8,000–$15,000+ for premium Crimsafe throughout. The exact cost depends on the number of doors and windows, mesh type, and brand chosen.
Are security screens worth the investment?
Yes. Security screens provide a strong return on investment through insurance premium savings ($100–$300/year), energy bill reductions (~$120/year from reduced cooling costs), and increased property value ($10,000–$20,000). Over a 15-year lifespan, quality screens typically pay for themselves while providing ongoing security and comfort.
What is the cheapest type of security screen door?
The most affordable security screen doors are standard aluminium diamond grille doors, costing $400–$700 installed in Brisbane. While they provide basic security and meet Australian standards, they don't offer the same level of protection or aesthetics as stainless steel mesh screens. For better security at a moderate price, non-branded stainless steel mesh doors range from $700–$1,200.
