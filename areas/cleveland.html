Security Screens & Blinds in Cleveland

Coastal protection specialists — salt-air resistant screens for bayside homes

Coastal Protection Specialists

Cleveland's proximity to Moreton Bay means salt air is a real concern for every home in the suburb. Standard aluminium and 304 grade stainless steel screens corrode faster in coastal conditions, leading to premature failure and costly replacements.

We recommend 316 marine grade stainless steel mesh for all Cleveland installations. It's the same grade used in marine hardware and coastal infrastructure — built to withstand the salt-laden sea breeze that comes off the bay day after day.

Cleveland-Specific Concerns

Salt Spray Corrosion

Within 5km of the coast, 316 stainless steel is essential. 304 grade and aluminium will pit and corrode, weakening security and appearance within a few years.

High Humidity

Cleveland's coastal humidity accelerates wear on inferior mesh materials. Marine grade stainless steel resists moisture-driven oxidation far better than standard alternatives.

Sandflies & Midges

The sea breeze brings sandflies and midges. We offer finer mesh options that block these smaller insects while still allowing good airflow and visibility.

Unobstructed Views

Waterfront and elevated properties need screens that don't block the bay views. Invisi-Gard style perforated mesh provides security while maintaining clear sightlines.

Why 316 Marine Grade?

316 stainless steel contains molybdenum, which gives it superior resistance to chloride pitting — the main cause of corrosion in coastal environments. In Cleveland's salt air, 316 SS lasts 20+ years, while inferior 304 grade or aluminium may show visible corrosion in as little as 5–10 years.

We always recommend 316 marine grade for any property within 5km of Moreton Bay. It costs more upfront but saves significantly on replacements over the life of your home.
