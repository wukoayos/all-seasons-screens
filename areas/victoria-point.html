Security Screens & Blinds in Victoria Point

New builds, renovations, and premium upgrades for one of Brisbane’s fastest-growing suburbs.

New Developments Need Screens From Day One

Victoria Point is one of the Redlands’ fastest-growing suburbs, with new housing estates and townhouse developments appearing every year. If you’re building or have recently moved into a new home, screens should be part of your first fit-out — not an afterthought.

We work with builders and homeowners during the construction phase to ensure seamless integration. Screens can be measured from architectural plans and installed as soon as the frames are in, so you’re protected from the day you move in.

Upgrade Builder-Grade Screens

Many new Victoria Point homes come with basic aluminium grille screens that offer minimal security. A popular upgrade is replacing these builder-grade screens with premium 316-marine-grade stainless steel mesh — stronger, better looking, and genuinely secure. The upgrade pays for itself in deterrence value alone.

Large Sliding & Bi-Fold Doors

Modern Victoria Point homes feature large sliding and bi-fold doors opening onto outdoor living areas. We supply stacking and bi-fold security screen configurations that match these openings exactly — full security without compromising the indoor-outdoor flow that Queensland living demands.

Blinds for New Homes

Fitting out a new home? We supply and install a full range of blinds alongside your security screens. Roller blinds for clean, modern lines. Honeycomb (cellular) blinds for energy efficiency — their insulating air pockets can reduce cooling costs significantly during Brisbane’s hot summers.

Order screens and blinds together and we’ll measure and install everything in one visit, saving you time and ensuring a coordinated look throughout your home.

Local Knowledge Matters

Based in nearby Capalaba, we’ve been fitting screens across Victoria Point for over 25 years. We know the local builders, the common frame types, and the environmental conditions — from coastal salt exposure to summer storm season. Every product we install is selected to handle these conditions long-term.
