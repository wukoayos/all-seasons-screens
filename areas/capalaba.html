About Capalaba Homes

Capalaba has a great mix of older established homes from the 1970s and 80s alongside newer developments. This means we see a wide range of screen and blind needs across the suburb.

Common jobs in Capalaba include replacing aging aluminium diamond grilles with modern stainless steel mesh security screens, fitting fly screens to combat the humid subtropical climate and its mosquitoes, and installing blinds for west-facing windows that cop the afternoon sun. Many older homes also need door and window frames measured for non-standard sizes — something we handle daily.

Whether you're in the established streets near Capalaba Central or in the newer estates, we know the housing stock and can recommend the right product for your home and budget.
