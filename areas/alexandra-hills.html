Security Screens & Blinds in Alexandra Hills

Child safety screens, energy-efficient blinds, and modern security upgrades for established family homes.

Child Safety Is the Priority

Alexandra Hills is one of the Redlands’ most established family suburbs. With many homes built on elevated blocks and featuring multi-level designs, child safety window screens are not optional — they’re essential. Our window screens comply with AS 5203, the Australian standard for window safety devices, giving you confidence that your children are protected.

These screens serve double duty: keeping children safe while also providing security against break-ins and keeping insects out. One screen, three problems solved.

Energy-Efficient Blinds

Alexandra Hills homes cop plenty of afternoon sun, especially west-facing windows. Honeycomb (cellular) blinds trap air in insulating pockets, reducing heat transfer by up to 34%. That translates directly to lower air conditioning bills through Brisbane’s long summers. We supply honeycomb blinds in blockout and light-filtering options to suit every room.

Replacing Old Diamond Grille

Many Alexandra Hills homes still have original diamond grille security screens from the 1970s–90s. These old-style grilles are easy to lever apart and offer poor protection by modern standards. Replacing them with stainless steel mesh security screens provides genuine resistance to forced entry, a cleaner appearance, and better airflow.

A Mix of Home Styles

Alexandra Hills has everything from 1970s brick-and-tile homes to modern infill townhouses. We’ve been fitting screens to all of them for over 25 years. Older homes often need non-standard sizing — we custom-measure every opening rather than relying on standard sizes, so you get a perfect fit regardless of your home’s age.

For newer sections of the suburb, we work with builders and developers to fit screens during construction, ensuring seamless integration with modern window and door systems.

Your Local Screen Specialists

Our workshop is just down the road in Capalaba, making Alexandra Hills one of our closest service areas. Short travel distance means faster turnaround on quotes and installation. We measure, manufacture, and install everything ourselves — no subcontractors, no delays.
